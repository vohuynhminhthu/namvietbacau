Policy supporting Enokitake mushroom dealers on PR - Marketing

• Agents will be supported documents, advertising signs at the sales location.
• Partner information will be advertised on the website, and in our marketing programs.

Order policy

• If ordering mushrooms in bulk, partners must order at least 1 month before the time of picking up.
• Bac Au Mushroom is committed to providing clean mushrooms, meeting food hygiene and safety standards. Each product has a clear traceability stamp.
• Price list for distributors excluding shipping fees. Shipping costs will be agreed between the sales department and partners depending on the region and order value.



Other support

• Bac Au Mushroom commits to having only one distributor in each region to help maximize profits for partners.
• We are ready to introduce large customers near the region to purchase at distribution partners.
