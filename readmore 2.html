Enoki mushroom preservation technique

Uses:

Mushrooms contain high amounts of zinc and potassium, so it is very effective for the elderly and patients with hypertension. This mushroom also contains substances that have anti-cancer effects very effectively. Enoki mushroom is one of the mushrooms with very high nutritional value. It is estimated that in 100 g of dried enoki mushrooms contain more than 31 g of protid and 6 g of lipid. It also contains many vitamins such as B1, B2, C, E, PP and amino acids necessary for the body, in particular, the Enoki mushroom has more lysine (content is 2 times higher than fat mushrooms), very Essential for growth, height, and intelligence in children. Therefore, this kind of edible mushroom is also known as "Increase her mind" (mushrooms enhance mental power).


Methods of preservation:

If you store Enoki mushroom at a temperature of about 1-5 degrees Celsius, you can use them within 45 days. If exposed to normal temperature, you can only use shiitake for 1-3 days.

Put the Enoki mushroom into a pot, fill the refrigerator with water and submerge. This way will preserve mushrooms for 3-4 days.

Best to use right after buying.
