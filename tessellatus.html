Hypsizygus tessellatus

Hypsizygus tessellatus contains many minerals and amino acids that enhance memory, improve the nervous system. In addition, Hypsizygus tessellatus also helps to strengthen the immune system and prevent cancer. Hypsizygus tessellatus is suitable for cooking hot pot or making stir-fries with seafood is also very delicious.


Hypsizygus tessellatus has a body length of 4 -8cm, fat, lower body white and browning up to the hat. Mushroom caps are relatively small in size, have a pattern and are brown.


Hypsizygus tessellatus grows under conditions of 12 - 18oC so it is mainly cultivated in industry, very little naturally. Shiitake mushrooms are rich in vitamins and minerals that are good for the body, helping to strengthen the mind, prevent cancer and slow down the aging process.
