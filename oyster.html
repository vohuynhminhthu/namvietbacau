Oyster mushroom

Oyster mushroom is a fresh mushroom rich in nutrients and medicinal properties, so it is grown on straw, bagasse, sawdust ... This mushroom has the effect of detoxifying and protecting liver cells, can fight cancer and antiviral. , reduces the risk of cardiovascular diseases ...


Gray Oyster mushroom has a delicious flavor, brittle mushroom meat and is tougher than white abalone mushroom. This is a great food used to process many different dishes for the family, both delicious and healthy. Oyster mushroom products are carefully selected from clean mushrooms, with the prestigious Bac Au Mushroom brand specializing in supplying fresh mushrooms of all kinds to the domestic market, helping consumers to be assured of their choice.


Nutrition and health benefits of Gray Oyster mushroom:

Gray Oyster mushroom is 100% grown in Vietnam, following a standard production process, free of harmful chemicals and pesticide residues to ensure food safety and hygiene. Gray Oyster mushroom has firm flesh, thick, sweet aroma and certain toughness. According to Western Medicine, Gray Oyster mushroom has many nutritional values ​​such as vitamins B, C, D and folic acid, which help replenish the body's abundant energy sources and enhance health. Gray Oyster mushroom also helps with blood sugar levels thanks to the compound lovastatin, thereby limiting myocardial ischemia.


In addition, mushrooms are especially beneficial for people with diabetes because they contain compounds that lower blood cholesterol and reduce the risk of diabetes.


Preserving and processing Gray Oyster mushroom:

Gray Oyster mushroom should be stored at a temperature of 5 -15 degrees C, shelf life of about 12 days from the date of packaging. Oyster mushroom should be soaked with diluted saline before processing. Note that you shouldn't eat too much because Oyster mushroom can cause indigestion.


Oyster mushroom is often used to make many delicious and nutritious dishes for families such as Fried Oyster mushroom with beef, fried abalone mushrooms. In particular, Gray Oyster mushroom is very popular by vegetarians, patients with diabetes, gout, blood fat ...
