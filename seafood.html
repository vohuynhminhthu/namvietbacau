Seafood mushroom

Seafood Mushroom, also known as White Beech Mushroom, is white and tastes like seafood.


They contain many minerals, amino acids and many other nutrients that enhance memory, improve the nervous system.


In addition, seafood mushrooms also have the ability to boost immunity, help inhibit and prevent cancer and many other diseases.


Seafood mushrooms also have anti-aging effects, enhance liver function and are very good for the stomach.


Food can be used to prepare many delicious and attractive dishes such as stir-fried with beef, chicken, seafood ... or served with hotpot is also very delicious.
