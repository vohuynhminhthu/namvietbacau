About Us

Nam Viet Bac Au is the result of a cooperation between our Bac Au Hi-Tech Food Joint Stock Company and our Global Viet Food Company Ltd.


Bac Au High Technology Food Joint Stock Company operates in the field of growing and trading edible mushrooms such as Enokitake mushroom, Japanese King Oyster mushroom, Shiitake mushroom, King Oyster mushroom, ...


From the beginning, Bac Au Mushroom has boldly invested in a High-Tech Mushroom Manufacturing Plant in Dak Lak Province. With an investment of more than 4 million USD, this place is considered by Japanese experts to be one of the most modern mushroom factories in Southeast Asia.


Besides advanced production lines, Bac Au Mushroom also focuses on using clean raw materials in production. We absolutely do not use chemicals or growth stimulants. By applying strict quality control standards, the Bac Au mushroom product has gained FDA certification for food imported from the United States.
