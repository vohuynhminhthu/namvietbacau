Enoki mushroom

Great use of Enoki mushrooms:

Enoki mushroom currently on the market is mostly self-grown, white, long and thin stems, soft sweet taste. This type of mushroom is known for its use as: Regulating blood pressure, detoxifying heat, increasing vitality for men, improving health...


In addition, enoki mushrooms are useful for the elderly, people with high blood pressure. This mushroom contains a substance with an effective anti-cancer effect. Enoki mushrooms are rich in lysine which is essential for the growth process, improving children's height and intelligence, lowering blood fat, preventing gastrointestinal ulcer disease and hepatobiliary disease.


How to choose to buy and use enoki mushrooms

• Choose to buy Enoki mushrooms indicated on the packaging about the expiry date and origin.
• Choose to buy mushrooms that are still fresh, not crushed, the roots are not separated or peeled off, especially mushrooms without oily water.
• Need to look carefully on the product supplier packaging to avoid buying poor quality Enoki mushrooms.
• Acupuncture needles if refrigerated at 1-5 degrees Celsius, can be used within 45 days. If the temperature is normal, depending on the weather conditions, you only use acupuncture needles 1-3 days.
• Experts recommend using mushrooms immediately after buying. And note that mushrooms need to be cooked, avoiding the consumption of raw, undercooked mushrooms.
