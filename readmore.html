Enoki mushroom rolls with
oyster sauce

Material:

1 pack of Enoki mushroom of Bac Au

Beef 300grams

1 minced garlic

Oyster sauce 3 tablespoons

Onions, leaves, ground pepper, garlic, chili


Making:

1. Needles cut feet clean and drain. Sliced beef (large board). Put the needle mushroom into the rolled beef piece, use the green onion to fasten.

2. Add oil to pan, sauté garlic until fragrant, add mushroom to roll beef and sauté for 2-3 minutes, add 3 tablespoons of oyster sauce and stir well on high heat for 1 minute, turn off the heat.

3. Serve on a plate, sprinkle with ground pepper on hot use.
