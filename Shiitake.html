How to choose delicious mushrooms and safe

Shiitake mushrooms are of three types commonly used in food processing. The best shiitake mushrooms are buttons, button-shaped, small feet, thick, glossy yellow, and palpable hands, under white compartments lined up together.


When soaked in water, the mushrooms bloom evenly but still chewy, the soaked water has a golden hue and a sweet fragrance.


Note:

Absolutely do not choose moist or strange-smelling mushrooms. When you press on the "canopy" of the mushroom, then remove it while sniffing it, if the aroma is pure, it is a delicious mushroom. The top surface of either yellow or white is a safe, chemical-free shiitake.
