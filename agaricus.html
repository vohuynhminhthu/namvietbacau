Agaricus bisporus

Agaricus bisporus is one of the popular edible mushrooms, many people prefer. Not only has a high nutritional value, Agaricus bisporus has also been used to prevent disease.


What is agaricus bisporus?

Agaricus bisporus is the scientific name of this fungus.


Agaricus bisporus is the most commonly grown edible mushroom in the world because it is easy to grow. This mushroom is used to make many delicious dishes with many uses for health.


What is the shape of agaricus bisporus?

• Mushroom hats are shaped like a button with a round cap and sometimes have small scales, hemispherical, thick, bright white. Mushroom caps with a diameter of 5-10 cm. • Cylindrical mushroom body 5-6 cm high. • Mushroom legs are short and firm, usually only 3 cm high and 1.5-2 cm thick.

What are the uses of Agaricus bisporus?

Nutritional value of Agaricus bisporus:

It is estimated that 100g of fresh mushrooms contains a good amount of protid - a protein that is very good for the body. In mushrooms also have a good amount of fiber, ash, Ca, P, Fe, vitamins B1, B2, C needed. In addition, mushrooms also contain many precious amino acids such as threonine, alanine, aspartic acid, leucine, citrulline, glycine, glutamic acid, sarcosine, proline ... and many other trace elements such as Na, K, Mn, Zn, Cu.


Uses of Agaricus bisporus according to traditional medicine:

According to the research of traditional medicine, mushrooms have a sweet taste, coolness, have the utility of spleen of qi, leap of phlegm, eliminating phlegm, and are suitable for those who have anorexia fatigue due to weak spleen. , women with milk deficiency, chronic bronchitis, chronic hepatitis, leukopenia syndrome ...


Uses of Agaricus bisporus according to modern medicine:

Japanese scientists extracted Agaricus bisporus into a substance, called PS-K. This substance has anti-cancer effects, enhances the body's immune capacity. Scientists who have conducted clinical trials on breast and skin cancers have shown quite good results.


Numerous studies of nutrition experts suggest that taking nAgaricus bisporus as a daily food or drinking this mushroom extract can treat chronic hepatitis and leukopenia. Especially effective when used in conjunction with Schisandra, can reach 73%. In addition, Agaricus bisporus also works to lower blood sugar, lower serum cholesterol levels and improve pancreatic function. Therefore, this mushroom is one of the ideal foods for people with cardiovascular disease, diabetes, cancer and pancreatic disease.
