Lingzhi mushroom

Lingzhi mushroom is an herb used in Eastern medicine for a long time. In the traditional medicine of China, Vietnam, Japan, Korea ... all mention this herb.


The effects of Lingzhi mushroom include:

• Helps boost the immune system • Treatment of viral infections such as influenza, swine flu and avian influenza • Treatment of lung-related diseases such as asthma and bronchitis • Treat heart disease and related diseases like high blood pressure and high cholesterol • Treatment of kidney disease • Cancer treatment • Treatment of liver diseases • Increase testosterone levels • Blood thinning • Dilates blood vessels • Reduce blood sugar • Good for skin and hair

In addition, the use of Lingzhi mushroom is also mentioned in the treatment of:

• HIV/AIDS • The disease is afraid of heights • Chronic fatigue syndrome • Insomnia • Stomach ulcers • Poisoning • Herpes • Reduce stress, sedate and relax muscles.

Combined with other herbs, the effects of Lingzhi mushroom are also used to treat prostate cancer.



Mechanism of action:

Because of the many health benefits, Lingzhi mushroom is considered to be an herb that has anti-tumor (cancer) and immune system benefits.


Currently, the use of Lingzhi mushroom has not been fully studied. Therefore, you should consult a physician before use.



Ingredient:

Ingredients of Lingzhi mushroom include: protein, fat, carbohydrate, fiber, some vitamins and minerals like with potassium, calcium, phosphorus, magnesium, selenium, iron, zinc, copper ... In addition, mushrooms also contain many Biologically active molecules such as terpenoids, steroids, phenols, nucleotides and their derivatives, glycoproteins and polysaccharides. Mushroom protein contains all the essential amino acids, especially rich in lysine and leucine.



Dosage of Lingzhi mushroom:

The dosage of Lingzhi mushroom will depend on your age, health status and other issues of concern. Lingzhi mushroom may be unsafe. Discuss this with your physician and doctor to see if you can use this herb and the appropriate dosage.



Dosage forms:

GLingzhi mushroom is usually prepared in such forms as: tea, flour, liquid extract.



How to use Lingzhi mushroom effectively:

Lingzhi mushroom has a slightly bitter taste, is difficult to drink. Therefore, depending on the purpose of use you can use in combination with licorice, artichoke, honey or other herbs. The use of Ganoderma in traditional medicine is often used in the following forms:


Use whole mushrooms to drink water instead of water daily:

You take 50g of Lingzhi mushroom to wash away dirt, put in a kettle to cook with 1 liter of water, boil for 2-3 minutes and then turn off the fire. Leave it warm for about 5 - 10 minutes, then light on the small burner and continue to cook. Cook until the water runs out to about 800ml then squeeze out the water. Pour the mushrooms out of the kettle to cool down, use a knife or scissors to cut the mushrooms and then pour the water into it to cook again. After 3 cooking times, you will have more than 2 liters of Lingzhi mushroom water. Cool water, you pour into the bottle, to cool in the refrigerator to drink gradually throughout the day. You can take advantage of Lingzhi mushroom residues by drying and boiling bath water. This water is good for skin and hair.


Grind the whole mushroom into powder and then brake with water like brake tea:

Put the Lingzhi mushroomi powder in the teapot and brake with very boiling water, wait for 5 - 10 minutes and drink the residue. You may find it a little uncomfortable to drink, but as recommended by scientists, this will help you take full advantage of the benefits of Lingzhi mushroom.


Pickled wine:

You take 200g of dried mushrooms, leave or slice as you like, soak with 2 liters of alcohol (alcohol about 39 degrees) for 30 days to use. Note that Lingzhi mushroom soaked in wine for as long as possible. Should drink this wine after dinner, each drink 1 to 2 small glasses (form of eye-buffalo glasses).


Grind into fine powder and mix with honey to nourish skin: Lingzhi mushroom powder mixed with honey makes a very good skin mask.
